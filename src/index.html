About Me

Hey there! I'm Clayton, a 20 year old computer science student who is also learning web development. I'm a semester away from being a junior at Old Dominion University, and I'm passionate about learning new technologies and building projects that solve real-world problems. When it comes to libraries/frameworks, I mainly use React and Next.js, but I'm currently learning Svelte and SvelteKit. In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, collecting vinyl, spending time with family and friends, cycling outdoors, and coding. I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a developer, so after taking a look at my work, feel free to email me!
